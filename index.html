Our Vision

Based in Port Elizabeth, South Africa, Sletcher Systems is more than just a technology company – we're a catalyst for change. Our mission is to leverage technology to transform local businesses and industries while delivering world-class solutions globally.

Founded by a passionate self-taught data scientist and machine learning engineer, we combine South African ingenuity with global expertise to craft innovative solutions that matter. With a deep understanding of grassroots challenges and enterprise-level demands, we bridge the digital divide to create meaningful impact.

At Sletcher Systems, we empower businesses of all sizes. Whether you're a local startup or a large corporation, we deliver tailored solutions to drive growth, efficiency, and innovation.
